Book Four / The Awakened Mind

As Above, So Below

The 7 Hermetic Principles Explained Through the Lens of Black Progress and Liberation

The 7 Hermetic Principles, Mentalism, Correspondence, Vibration, Polarity, Rhythm, Cause and Effect, and Gender, are among the oldest frameworks for understanding how the universe operates. This book brings them out of the esoteric and into the practical, reading each principle through the lived experience of Black Americans. From the principle that mind creates reality, to the law that every cause produces an effect, this book shows how ancient wisdom applies directly to progress, liberation, and the disciplined building of power.
