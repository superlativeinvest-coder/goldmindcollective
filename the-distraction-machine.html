The Distraction Machine

How Entertainment, Social Media, and Culture Were Engineered to Keep Us Occupied and Off Balance

You are not distracted by accident. From the rise of television in Black households to the architecture of social media feeds, this book documents how attention became the most valuable commodity in the world, and how Black attention specifically has been captured, monetized, and redirected away from building.
